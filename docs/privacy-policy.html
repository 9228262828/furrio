1. Introduction

Furrio is a simple pet profile management application that helps users organize information about their pets in a private and easy-to-use environment. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled while using the application.

Furrio is designed with privacy in mind and works primarily using local device storage without requiring online accounts.

2. Purpose of the Application

Furrio allows users to create and manage basic pet profiles including names, pet types, age, weight, favorite status, and personal notes for household pets.

3. Information You Enter

The application stores only the information that you voluntarily add, including pet names, pet categories, age, weight, notes, and preferences.

4. Local Device Storage

All pet profiles are stored locally on your own device using local storage technologies. Furrio does not maintain cloud databases for your information.

5. No User Account

Furrio does not require account registration, login credentials, usernames, passwords, or email verification.

6. No Personal Information Collection

The application does not intentionally collect your name, address, phone number, location, contacts, payment information, government identifiers, or similar personal information.

7. Pet Information

Pet information entered into the application remains under your control and is used solely to provide profile management features.

8. Offline Usage

Most Furrio features work completely offline without requiring an internet connection.

9. No Cloud Synchronization

Furrio does not automatically synchronize your pet profiles with cloud storage services or external databases.

10. No Analytics

The application does not use analytics SDKs or usage tracking services to monitor your activity.

11. No Advertising

Furrio contains no advertisements, advertising identifiers, or advertising SDK integrations.

12. No Third-Party Sharing

Your pet information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties by Furrio.

13. Device Permissions

Furrio is designed to operate without requesting unnecessary sensitive permissions. Future optional features may request additional permissions only when required.

14. Data Security

Because information remains on your own device, security primarily depends on your device protection, operating system, and screen lock configuration.

15. Data Retention

Your pet profiles remain stored until you edit them, delete them, clear application data, or uninstall the application.

16. Data Deletion

You may delete individual profiles inside the application or remove all locally stored information by clearing app storage or uninstalling Furrio.

17. Children's Privacy

Furrio is not specifically directed toward children and does not knowingly collect children's personal information.

18. Veterinary Disclaimer

Furrio is an organizational tool only and should not be considered veterinary, medical, nutritional, emergency, or professional pet care advice.

19. Future Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect application improvements, legal requirements, or store policy updates.

20. Acceptance

By continuing to use Furrio, you acknowledge that you have read and accepted this Privacy Policy.

21. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the contact information provided on the application's official store listing.
