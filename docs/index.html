About Furrio

Furrio helps you create simple pet profiles for your cats, dogs, birds, rabbits, fish, and other pets.

You can save basic details such as pet name, type, age, weight, favorite status, and notes in one clean place.

Privacy First

Furrio stores your pet profiles locally on your device. The app does not require login, accounts, backend servers, ads, analytics, or third-party data sharing.
